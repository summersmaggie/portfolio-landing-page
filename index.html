MAGGIE SUMMERS

Hello! Thanks for stopping by. I'm Maggie Summers. I'm not good at talking about myself, so I'll keep this short. I worked in social media for almost 5 years, but after becoming an expert in the field and becoming disillusioned with the industry, I decided I needed a fresh start. So, on January 2, 2018, I started at Epicodus to learn programming.Please take a look around to see what I've done so far. I'm looking forward to the opportunity to work together in the future.
